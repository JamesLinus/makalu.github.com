What we’ve done

Over the past decade, we’ve had the opportunity to contribute to projects large and small, across many technological and application domains. Here’s some highlights.

ChessDrop

We built a personal chess database to record, analyze, and annotate your games. Read the blog article for a great insight into the interaction design process.

Visit ChessDrop.com →

Rego

Our place bookmarking app took the world by storm when it launched in 2014. It was picked as one of the top apps by Apple and was featured on the App Store home page.

Visit RegoApp.com →

Marek Lieberberg Konzertagentur

For the past decade, we’ve been the design and tech team for the world’s fifth largest concert promoter.

Rock am Ring Website

We created an innovative web app for Marek Lieberberg that from a single code base drives three separate public festival websites and apps, while being managed from a single CMS.

Visit rock-am-ring.com →

Rock am Ring iOS/Android App

To provide a consistent cross-platform user experience, we made extensive use of webviews in the MLK iOS and Android mobile apps, and relying on native technology for areas like venue mapping.

Multi-Festival CMS

We created an CMS to allows MLK staff to completely manage and deploy three independent festival websites and mobile apps.

Marek Lieberberg CMS

The CMS we developed and extended over a five year period helps the world’s fourth largest concert promoter to market and collaborate on nearly 1,000 events per year.
