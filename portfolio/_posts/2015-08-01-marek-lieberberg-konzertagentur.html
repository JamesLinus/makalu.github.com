Since first developing the content management system that drives the corporate website in 2006, we've gone on to build dozens of websites, internal tools, and mobile apps for Marek Lieberberg Konzertagentur.

Our custom music festival CMS drives the websites and mobile apps for Rock am Ring, Rock im Park, and Rock'n'Heim, scaling to serve hundreds of thousands of requests in the peak hours.

In August of 2015 MLK was acquired by the world's largest event promoter, Live Nation.
