RaceSplitter is an innovative system comprised of an iOS app and companion web service that allows “Do it yourself” timing of sports events. Over 1.3 million participants have been timed with RaceSplitter, in nearly 100 countries. The screenshots below are from the design of version 2 of the platform, and reflect our view of how to efficiently organize a multi-race event, how to perform timing in an error-free way, and how to make the organizer the star of the day.

For individuals, ChessDrop provides a cloud-based solution for recording, storing and analyzing their chess games, available anytime, anywhere and on any device. For clubs and academies, ChessDrop provides a modern, low-cost means of providing chess instruction and helping students develop the habit of post-game analysis.

The screenshots below demonstrate some of the visual and interactive innovations we’ve brought to digital chess, including dynamically shifting background color weights to indicate current positional evaluation, and a usable method of displaying and navigating sub-variations and move annotations. Particularly challenging was the design of such a complex, interactive environment, in a way that works on desktop, smartphones and tablets, in both landscape and portrait orientations.
